Billing System Tutorial & FAQ

Getting Started

First, sign up for an account by selecting your role (Business or Client).
Log in with your credentials to access your dashboard.
Tip: Make sure to remember which role you selected, as it determines your available features.

For Business Users

Managing Billing Entries

Click "Add New Entry" to create billing entries for services or products.
Fill in the service name, quantity, rate, and optional description.
Entries can be edited or deleted later if needed.

Creating Invoices

Click "Generate Invoice" to create a new invoice.
Select a client from the dropdown menu.
Set the payment terms (due days).
Add items either manually or select from your existing billing entries.
Review and generate the invoice - a PDF will be created automatically.
Tip: You can view all generated invoices in your dashboard and track their status.

For Clients

View all invoices assigned to you in your client dashboard.
Download or view invoice PDFs directly from the dashboard.
Track payment status and due dates for each invoice.
Tip: Keep track of your invoice due dates to ensure timely payments.

Profile Management

Access your profile settings by clicking "Profile" in the dashboard.
Update your username if needed.
Change your password for security purposes.

Common Questions

How do I view invoice details?

Click the "View Details" button next to any invoice to see complete information including client details, amounts, and due dates.

Can I modify an invoice after creation?

Once an invoice is generated, it cannot be modified. You'll need to create a new invoice if changes are needed.

How do I handle payments?

Currently, the system tracks invoice status but does not process payments. Payment processing should be handled outside the system.
